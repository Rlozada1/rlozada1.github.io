What is a complex number?

A complex number is define as having two part: a real and imaginary parts. The real part is denoted by x while the imaginary part is defined by iy. Together they are defined by the sum z=x+iy.

Another major concept in these first sections how complex numbers behave in terms of operations. What we find is that complex numbers behave similiar to regular intergers. The concept of graphing is also spoken of which turns out to be easier with the definition given of a complex number(i.e the real axis is the x axis and the imaginay axis is the y axis)
